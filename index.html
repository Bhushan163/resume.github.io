Introduction

Project: Customer Onboarding SDK for Insurance Companies

Company: Arya.ai

Duration: October 2022 - December 2023

Team: UX/UI Designer, Developers, Product Managers

Clients: Initially it was only for ICICI Lombard but now it is being used by TATA AIG, Reliance Digital Insurance, Future Generali Insurance, Liberty General Insurance, Mahindra Insurance Brokers Ltd

Scope: Millions of users nationwide

Problem Statement

The Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) mandated the use of Know Your Customer (KYC) documents like PAN, AADHAAR, CKYC, and other Proof of Identity (POI) and Proof of Address (POA) documents for customer onboarding by insurance companies starting from January 2023. Our company embarked on a mission to create an SDK that streamlines and simplifies the onboarding process for these insurance giants while ensuring a seamless experience for millions of users across India.

Research Phase

User Research

  1. Stakeholder Interviews: Conducted interviews with key stakeholders at each insurance company to understand their unique onboarding challenges, regulatory concerns, and technology constraints.
  2. User Surveys: Administered surveys to policyholders and insurance agents to gather insights into their expectations, pain points, and preferences during the onboarding journey.

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed existing solutions in the market to identify gaps and opportunities. Studied best practices in the insurance industry and relevant SDKs in other sectors for inspiration.

UI Design

1. Visual Identity

Created a professional and trustworthy visual identity consistent with the branding of our client insurance companies.

2. Color Scheme

Utilized colors that convey trust and security, adhering to industry standards. Also maintained to symmetry of clients UI design.

Development Phase

Collaborated closely with developers to ensure seamless implementation of the designed solution. Continuous design reviews, code handoffs, and close communication facilitated a smooth development process.

Testing and Iteration

Conducted extensive usability testing with insurance agents and policyholders to identify and address usability issues, bottlenecks, and any discrepancies in the onboarding process. Iterative design adjustments were made based on user feedback.

Results

User Adoption

The SDK seamlessly integrated into the onboarding workflows of all six major insurance companies, causing minimal disruption and accelerating their customer acquisition processes.

Enhanced Efficiency

Insurance agents reported a remarkable reduction in onboarding time, resulting in increased productivity and higher customer satisfaction.

Future Enhancements

  1. Biometric Verification: Implement biometric authentication for enhanced security.
  2. Multi-Language Support: Expand language support to cater to a more diverse user base.
  3. Integration with e-Signatures: Enable electronic signatures for a fully paperless onboarding experience.

Conclusion

The Customer Onboarding SDK for Insurance Companies successfully addressed the regulatory requirements imposed by IRDAI while delivering a seamless and user-centric experience for both insurance agents and policyholders. Our commitment to ongoing feedback and future enhancements ensures that the SDK remains a valuable asset, poised to evolve in response to the ever-changing landscape of the insurance industry.

Introduction

Project Overview

The AI API Marketplace is an online platform designed to provide users of all ages and backgrounds with a simple and accessible way to explore, demo, subscribe to, and manage various AI APIs. This case study will focus on the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design process for this platform, with an emphasis on usability and accessibility. The platform also offers one-click login options using Gmail and Twitter to streamline the registration process.

Objective

Create a user-friendly, visually appealing, and intuitive platform that allows users to easily discover, try, subscribe to, and manage AI APIs.

Research Phase

User Personas

To better understand our target audience, we identified three user personas:

Ajay (Developer)
  • Age: 28
  • Background: Software developer
  • Goal: Find and integrate AI APIs into projects efficiently.
Esha (Student)
  • Age: 20
  • Background: Computer science student
  • Goal: Learn about AI technology through hands-on experience.
Ashutosh (Business Owner)
  • Age: 45
  • Background: Entrepreneur
  • Goal: Explore AI APIs for potential business applications.

User Needs

Based on user personas, we identified key user needs:

  • Discover APIs: Users want an easy way to find and explore different AI APIs.
  • Demo APIs: Users should be able to test APIs before subscribing.
  • Subscribe and Pay: Seamless subscription and payment processes.
  • Dashboard: An intuitive dashboard to manage API subscriptions.
  • One-Click Login: Quick registration using Gmail and Twitter.

Design Phase

Information Architecture

Homepage:
  • Dropdown in navigation for discovering APIs
  • Featured APIs section
  • One-click login options
API Listings:
  • Clear API descriptions
  • Demo buttons for testing
  • Subscription options and pricing
Dashboard:
  • List of subscribed APIs
  • Subscription management options
  • Billing and payment history
